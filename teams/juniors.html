Junior Teams

Rhos United have Junior Teams covering Under 7s up to Under 12s

Details of each of our junior teams can be found below. Each season the juniors teams enjoy a Christmas Meal together, a footgolf session, access to football tournaments and an end of season presentation event.

Under 12s

The Rhos United Under 12s team is coached by Andrew and Ross and train on Tuesdays and Fridays. They play matches on Saturdays.

Contact Under 12s

Under 11s - Red Devils

The Red Devils are coached by Gavin and Danny and train on Tuesdays and Thursdays. They play matches on Sundays.

Contact Red Devils

Under 11s - Rovers

The Rovers are coached by Chris and train on Wednesdays. They play matches on Sundays.

Contact Rovers

Under 11s - Red Stars

The Red Stars are coached by Steph and Neil and train on Tuesdays and Thursdays. They play matches on Sundays.

Contact Red Stars

Under 10s - Rapids

Under 9s - Rockets
