From Monday 8th April we will be starting football lessons for boys and girls currently in years 3 & 4 at Nant Y Groes school.

From Thursday 11th April we will be starting football lessons for boys and girls currently in years 5 & 6 at Brookfield Drive.

These sessions are aimed at new players to give them the opportunity to develop new skills, meet new friends and play the beautiful game we all love. Current Rhos United players are also welcome to attend and will get 50% off.

Years 3 & 4 - Nant Y Groes School - Mondays 5pm to 6pm - £2 per session
Years 5 & 6 - Brookfield Drive - Thursdays 6pm to 7:30pm - £3 per session
