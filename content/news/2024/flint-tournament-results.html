Well played to all the teams that entered the Flint Tournament today, hope everyone enjoyed themselves.

A massive congratulations to our Super U7's who won the tournament beating Hawarden on pentalties, well done boys and to the coaches Craig and Dan.

U11 Red Devils were defeated on penalties in the Europa League final against Llay from Wrexham, so unlucky not to bring another trophy home. Well done boys and coach Danny.

U11 Red Stars and U10 Rapids also played but unfortunately never got out of the group stage, but a massive well done and great experience for you in a high level tournament.

Thanks to all the coaches and parents for your time and commitment.
